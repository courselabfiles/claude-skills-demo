# My voice

## Procedure
1. Read the writing sample below.
2. Model its sentence length and rhythm, its vocabulary, its structure, and the things it never does (words it avoids, punctuation it avoids, openings it avoids).
3. Draft the requested text in that voice. Every draft is for human review. If a fact, a number, or a commitment is not supplied, stop and flag it rather than inventing it.
