# Board brief formatter

## Procedure
1. Read the input.
2. Use only what is stated. Do not infer or invent.
3. Sort each item into one of the five sections below.
4. If a section has nothing, write "none".

## Output format
Five sections and nothing else:
- Decisions made (with who decided)
- Owners and what they own
- Dates and deadlines
- Open questions
- Needs your attention
