Subject: Near miss at south pit

Team,

We had a near miss at the south pit on Tuesday. A light vehicle entered an
active blast zone. No one was hurt. That is luck, not process.

I have read the initial report. The gate control failed and the radio check was
skipped. Both are known controls. Both were bypassed.

I want three things. A full review of the gate interlocks by Friday. A refresh
of the blast-zone entry procedure for every crew. A written account of why the
radio check was skipped, without blame, so we fix the cause.

Safety numbers for the quarter are steady. Two lost-time injuries. TRIFR at
0.38. Steady is not the goal. Zero is the goal.

I will raise this at the board safety session. I will not soften it. The
controls existed and did not hold.

Report back to me by Friday at noon. Keep it factual. Tell me what failed, not
who failed.

—EM
